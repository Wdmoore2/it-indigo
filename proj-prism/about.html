Heritage, Nature, and Community.

Mission Statment

Mission Statement: We are dedicated to providing outdoor enthusiasts with an environmentally responsible gateway to the wilderness of the Buffalo National River.

Our Story:

Located in the heart of Ponca, Arkansas, Nature’s Elk Outfitters was born from a passion for the Buffalo National River—America’s first national river. We aren't just an outfitter; we are stewards of the Ozarks.

Our Commitment

Inclusivity: We believe the outdoors belong to everyone. Our services are designed to be accessible to seasoned paddlers and first-time visitors alike. Sustainability: We practice "Leave No Trace" principles to protect the river’s ecosystem. Local Quality: We exclusively use Buffalo Canoes, locally manufactured in Newton County, ensuring you have the most reliable equipment on the water.

Why Nature's Elk

We offer a seamless digital booking experience paired with deep local expertise. Whether you are here for the world-class elk watching in Boxley Valley or a peaceful float through the Ozarks, we provide the tools and the gear to make your trip unforgettable.
